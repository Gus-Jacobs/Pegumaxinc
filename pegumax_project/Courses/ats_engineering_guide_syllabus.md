# The ATS Engineering Guide

*A recruiter's-eye course on building resumes that survive automated screening and land in front of a human. Treat your resume like a system to be engineered—not a document to be decorated.*

---

## Module 1: How ATS Pipelines Actually Work
**Demystify the screening stack so you stop guessing and start optimizing against how the system really reads you.**

- The lifecycle of an application: parse → store → search → rank → surface to recruiter
- Major ATS platforms (Workday, Greenhouse, Taleo, Lever) and how their parsing differs
- Myths vs. reality: what ATS actually rejects automatically vs. what a human filters

---

## Module 2: Machine-Readable Parsing Constraints
**Format your resume so the parser extracts every field correctly instead of mangling your experience into noise.**

- Safe structure: single-column layouts, standard section headers, and parser-friendly fonts
- Format landmines: tables, text boxes, headers/footers, graphics, and columns that scramble parsing
- File format choice (.docx vs. PDF) and validating how your resume parses before you submit

---

## Module 3: Semantic Keyword Optimization
**Match the language of the job description with precision so search and ranking algorithms surface you to recruiters.**

- Extracting hard keywords and skills from a job description and mapping them to your resume
- Synonyms, acronyms, and spelled-out variants (e.g., "ML" and "Machine Learning") for full coverage
- Keyword placement and density: relevance without stuffing that triggers human distrust

---

## Module 4: Keyword-to-Match-Score Modeling
**Reverse-engineer the relevance scoring so you can measure and raise your match rate before applying.**

- How TF-IDF–style relevance and boolean recruiter searches rank candidates
- Calculating your match score against a posting and closing the highest-value gaps
- Using free match-rate tools to test, iterate, and benchmark resume versions

---

## Module 5: Framing Technical Impact
**Convert duties into quantified, high-signal achievements that both algorithms and hiring managers reward.**

- The action–context–metric formula for bullets that lead with measurable outcomes
- Quantifying impact (%, $, time, scale) even when you don't have perfect data
- Surfacing the right technical stack and scope so seniority reads instantly

---

## Module 6: Resume Architecture & Section Strategy
**Structure the document so the most relevant, rankable content sits exactly where parsers and recruiters look first.**

- Section ordering and a skills section engineered for both parsing and keyword coverage
- The 6-second recruiter scan: top-third real estate and visual hierarchy that survives plain text
- Reverse-chronological vs. hybrid formats and when each helps or hurts your ranking

---

## Module 7: Tailoring Applications at Scale
**Customize every application to its posting without spending hours, using a repeatable engineering workflow.**

- A modular "master resume" system: swappable bullet and skill blocks per role archetype
- A per-application tailoring checklist that takes minutes, not hours
- Version control and tracking so you know which resume variant went where

---

## Module 8: The Application Metadata Layer
**Optimize the fields and profiles around your resume that quietly influence whether it's ever seen.**

- Online application form fields, knockout questions, and screening-question strategy
- LinkedIn and profile optimization for recruiter sourcing and inbound search
- Cover letters and custom fields: when they're parsed, scored, or ignored

---

## Module 9: Testing, Measurement & Iteration
**Run your job search like an experiment—measure response rates and improve them systematically.**

- Building a tracking system: applications, variants, callbacks, and conversion rates
- Diagnosing a low response rate: parsing failure, keyword gap, or targeting mismatch
- A/B testing resume variants and iterating toward a higher interview rate

---

## Module 10: Beyond the Algorithm—The Human Handoff
**Engineer the transition from passing the bot to winning the recruiter, where the real decision happens.**

- What recruiters actually do with surfaced resumes and how to earn the callback
- Referrals and direct outreach as a parallel path that bypasses the funnel entirely
- Ethical optimization: staying truthful and avoiding tactics that get you blacklisted

---
