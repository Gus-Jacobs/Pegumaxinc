Four promises we
refuse to break

We're small, so we can build the way we'd want software built for us — and we listen when you tell us what to fix.

Genuinely fast

Local-first means no waiting on the cloud to feel instant.

Private by default

Your files and data stay on your device. No tracking, no resale.

Free where we can

Most apps are free. We only charge for ones that cost money to maintain and run.

Shaped by your feedback

Every app has feedback built in. You tell us what to build — we build it.
