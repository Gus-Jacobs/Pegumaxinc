Pegumax / About

A small team that puts users first

We're a tiny independent studio with one stubborn belief: powerful software should be accessible, private, and affordable for everyone — and shaped by the people who actually use it.

A small team, by design
Close to our users, fast to respond.
// our story

Built by a few people
who actually listen

Pegumax is a small team. Being small is the point — it keeps us close to the people who use our software and quick to act on what they tell us. That's why every app ships with feedback built in: we want you to voice your opinion and tell us what you'd like to see next.

We keep as much of our software free as we possibly can. We only ask for payment on the apps that genuinely cost money to maintain and run — and even then, we try to keep it fair. No surveillance tax, no dark patterns, no nickel-and-diming.

If you want to help us keep building, the best way is to use our apps, send us your feedback — and, if you can, support us with a donation.

We give back. We donate 10% of our profits to charities and nonprofits to help people who need it. So when you pay for one of our apps or donate, a real slice of that goes straight to doing good.
// users first

Your voice is built into every app

Feedback isn't an afterthought hidden in a support email — it's a feature. Report a bug, request a feature, or just tell us what you think, right from the apps and this site. We read every message.

// the journey

How we got here

JULY 2025

Pegumax LLC founded

We start the studio and ship our first tool — Movie Word Scanner (later renamed LucidCut) — while beginning work on the Student Suite.

DECEMBER 2025

Game Portal built

A single secure launcher to download, manage, and play games.

JANUARY 2026

Student Suite deployed

After months of building, the all-in-one companion launches on web, iOS, and Android.

MAY 2026

Apex Studio launches

Our local-first media suite ships, alongside a major Game Portal feature update.

NOW

Always building

Ongoing development of Scribe, LucidCut, and Contour. Deep testing and rich features take time — we don't cut corners.
